## Overview

Prichuuter ("the App") is a free arcade game available on web, iOS, and Android. This policy describes what data is collected, how it is used, and your choices.

---

## Data We Collect

The App does **not** require an account, login, or any personal information to play. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any user-submitted content.

The following data is collected automatically through third-party services:

### 1. Analytics (Google Analytics & Matomo)

We use Google Analytics and a self-hosted Matomo instance to understand how players interact with the game. This includes:

- **Usage data:** pages viewed, session duration, in-game events (game starts, game overs, scores)
- **Device information:** device type, operating system, browser, screen resolution
- **Approximate location:** country/region level (derived from IP address)

Google Analytics may use cookies or similar identifiers. Matomo is self-hosted at insights.pripec.com and configured for privacy-friendly analytics.

- Google Analytics privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Matomo privacy information: https://matomo.org/privacy-policy/

### 2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

- **Device advertising ID** (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)
- **Ad interaction data:** impressions, clicks
- **Device information:** device model, OS version

On iOS, the App requests App Tracking Transparency (ATT) authorization before accessing the advertising identifier.

- Google AdMob privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

---

## How Data Is Used

- **Analytics data** is used to improve gameplay, fix bugs, and understand which features are most used.
- **Advertising data** is used by Google AdMob to serve ads, including personalized ads based on your advertising ID (unless you opt out).

---

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is shared only with:

- **Google** (Analytics and AdMob) — as described above
- **Matomo** — self-hosted, data stays on our own servers

---

## Data Retention

- Google Analytics data is retained according to Google's standard retention settings.
- Matomo data is retained on our self-hosted server and periodically purged.
- No personal data is stored within the App itself. Game progress (scores, settings) is stored locally on your device via localStorage and is never transmitted.

---

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data through the App, please contact us.

---

## Your Choices

- **Opt out of personalized ads:** Adjust your device settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking; Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads)
- **Disable analytics cookies:** Use your browser's cookie settings (web version)
- **App Tracking Transparency (iOS):** You can deny tracking permission when prompted
